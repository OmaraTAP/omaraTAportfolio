The Great Tenochtitlan

As an assignment during the Spring 2021 semester, my professor had instructed the class that she wanted us to design a map of anything. It could be a make believe world in a book, a game, a country, anything at all. All she asked is for us to do is extenssive research in order to make our maps as accurate as possible. Personally I decided I wanted to go back to my roots and recreate a digital map of the gorgeous city Aztec city of Tenochtitlan.
